Our Story

Lix Distro was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, trendy clothing at affordable prices.

What started as a small boutique in Jakarta has grown into one of Indonesia's most popular fashion destinations. We pride ourselves on our carefully curated collections that blend international trends with local sensibilities.

Every piece in our collection is selected for its quality, comfort, and style. We work directly with manufacturers to ensure ethical production practices and fair wages for workers.
